RECIPES

INGREDIENTS

6-3/4 oz. (1-1/2 cups) all-purpose flour

1/2 tsp. baking powder

1/4 tsp. table salt

4 oz. (1/2 cup) unsalted butter, softened

3/4 cup granulated sugar

1 large egg

1 tsp. pure vanilla extract

2 Tbs. unsweetened cocoa powder

2 oz. semisweet chocolate (preferably high quality)

METHOD

Step 1

In a medium bowl, sift together the flour, baking powder, and salt. With an electric mixer, beat the butter and sugar until light and fluffy.

Step 2

Beat in the egg and vanilla, scrape down the sides of the bowl, and beat again until blended. Add the flour mixture, beating just until combined.

Step 3

Remove half the dough (about 9 oz.); set aside. Whisk the cocoa into the melted chocolate; add to the dough remaining in the mixing bowl and beat briefly to blend.

Step 4

Divide the chocolate and vanilla doughs into 8-pieces each, more or less the same size

Step 5

Recombine the pieces, alternately pressing the chocolate and vanilla pieces together, to create a marbled ball. On a lightly floured surface, roll and shape the ball into a 9-inch-long log.

Step 6

Heat the oven to 350Â°F. Cover your cookie sheets with parchment. Unwrap the firm dough and roll the log briefly on the counter to make it more evenly round. Bake for 9 to 11 minutes.
