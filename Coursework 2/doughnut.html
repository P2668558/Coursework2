RECIPES

INGREDIENTS

2 eggs

1/2 sugar

1/2 cup milk

1 tsp vanilla

3 tbsp unsalted butter

3 cups flour

1/2 tsp salt

2 tbsp baking powder

METHOD

Step 1

In the mixer, add 2 medium eggs and 1/2 cup caster sugar. Mix well until creamy.

Step 2

Add 1 tsp vanilla and 3 tbsp melted unsalted butter. Continue mixing. Next, add 3 cups plain flour, 1/2 tsp salt and 2 tsp baking powder. Continue blending.

Step 3

Add 1/2 cup milk whiles blending pause to add more plain flour. Repeat this for 3 times.

Step 4

You'll see a doughy texture. Knead for a few minutes until smooth. Once that down rest it for 15 minutes.

Step 5

Time to roll the dough. Use a rolling pin. Keep rolling until its thickness is 1cm. Shape it with a circle cutter creating an O shape.

Step 6

Once that's done rest it for 5 minutes. After that fry the dough on medium heat. You'll see a golden ring doughnut.
