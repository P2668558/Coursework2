RECIPES

INGREDIENTS

2 cups hot water

1 pack of jelly

1 pack of biscuits

1 litre of whole milk

sugar

cornflour

cardamom

6 Strawberries

METHOD

Step 1

Add 2 cups of hot water with a whole packet of jelly. Boil for 5 whole minutes. Next let it cool dow for 6 minutes.

Step 2

have a glass footed and a bowl to refrigerate for 30 whole minutes. Crush a packet of biscuits.

Step 3

Time to make the firni. Boil 1 litre of whole milk, add 2/3 cup sugar, 8 tbsp cornflour and a pinch of cardamom. Now stir until you'll get a think consistency.

Step 4

scoop into a tray and leave to cool and refrigerate for 1 hour.

Step 5

Now decorate, adding 1/2 cup strawberries on the side with biscuit scrums in-between. Laying it with firni. Top off that with jelly.
